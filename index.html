About Me

Hello! My name is Annie Heath. I'm 32 years old and I was born in the U.S. I'm originally from Florida but I've lived in Philadelphia, Los Angeles, and for two years I lived on a tour bus that went around the U.S. with a Shakespeare company. I have a theater degree from Florida State University. For about ten years I worked in film, tv, and theater, making props and doing set dressing. Three years ago I decided the theatre life was not for me and I moved to Japan to teach English. Japan was amazing! I really enjoyed learning about the culture, meeting the people, and especially eating the food. In 2020 I moved to Madrid, Spain to work in a bilingual secondary school.


I have tons of hobbies. I like learning languages, arts and crafts, cooking, baking, tending to my indoor garden, traveling, listening to podcasts, and playing video games.
